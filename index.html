Member of Technical Staff · xAI

Building reliable reinforcement learning systems for science, coding, and long-horizon intelligence.

I am a Member of Technical Staff at xAI, where I work on large-scale RL stability, sample efficiency, and self-play RL recipes, with an emphasis on systems that become more capable in science and coding settings.

Previously, I studied computer science at Stanford and worked at Microsoft, where my research spanned multimodal inference, embodied AI, long-horizon decision-making, healthcare AI, and representation learning. I care about evaluation, scalable training, and the practical design choices that make advanced learning systems more robust.
